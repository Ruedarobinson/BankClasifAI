1. Information we collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Personal information: for example, your email address and basic account details when you register or contact us.
  • Non-personal / technical information: usage and diagnostic data (e.g., browser type, device, pages visited, access date/time, performance events), generally associated with cookies or similar technologies.
Important note about bank statements

BankClasifAI is designed to extract visible transaction fields such as date, description, and amount, convert them into JSON, and categorize them into accounting-style categories. We do not request banking passwords or online banking credentials.

2. Data you provide when using the service

  • Uploaded files: PDF or image statements you choose to process.
  • Structured outputs: extracted results (JSON) and generated reports (Excel/PDF).
  • User corrections: category/subcategory adjustments to improve organization.

3. Information collected automatically (cookies & analytics)

To operate and improve the service, we may use cookies (persistent or session) and similar technologies. These help us remember preferences, maintain sessions, measure performance, and prevent abuse.

  • Technical data (approximate IP, browser, device, operating system).
  • Usage events (pages, clicks, errors, load times).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some cookies are required for the service to work properly.

4. How we use your information

We use information to:

  • Provide the service: extraction, structuring (JSON), categorization, and export.
  • Support: respond to questions, incidents, and requests.
  • Security: prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and attacks.
  • Product improvement: aggregated analysis for performance and user experience.

5. AI and data processing

BankClasifAI uses AI to categorize transactions into accounting-style categories. Our approach is to minimize sensitive data use and process only what is necessary to deliver results.

We do not send confidential data to the AI

We design our workflow to avoid sending unnecessary confidential information to the AI. The goal is for the AI to work with minimal inputs (for example, description text and relevant signals) to assign an accounting category.

Note: Depending on your statement file, additional printed details may appear. We recommend reviewing and, if desired, masking/cropping information before uploading. We still apply controls and security measures to protect your information.
