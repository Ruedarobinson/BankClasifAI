Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BankClasifAI and how AI-powered bank statement classification works.

About the platform

What is BankClasifAI?
BankClasifAI is a platform that transforms bank statements into clear and organized financial data. It uses artificial intelligence to automatically classify income and expenses from PDF files or images.
Who is BankClasifAI for?
It is designed for individuals, freelancers, small businesses, entrepreneurs, and accounting firms that need to organize bank transactions without manual processes.
Do I need accounting knowledge to use it?
No. BankClasifAI is built to be simple and intuitive. You don’t need accounting or technical knowledge to use the platform.

Privacy & security

Is my banking data secure?
Yes. BankClasifAI does not share sensitive data with external AI models. We only process the minimum information required to classify transactions.
Is my data sent to AI systems?
Sensitive data such as full account numbers or personal identifiers is never shared. Data is anonymized before classification.
Where is my data stored?
We use secure Google Cloud infrastructure and follow industry standards for data protection, including periodic security audits.

Artificial intelligence

How does the AI classify transactions?
The AI analyzes transaction descriptions, amounts, and dates to identify patterns and assign accounting categories automatically.
Is the classification fully automatic?
Yes. The process is automatic, but you can always review results before exporting them.

Free trial & demo

Can I try BankClasifAI for free?
Yes. We offer a free demo so you can explore the platform before uploading real financial data.
Can I upload real bank statements in the demo?
We recommend using sample or non-sensitive statements during the demo.

Data export

Can I export the results?
Yes. You can export categorized income and expenses to Excel or PDF to share with your accountant or accounting software.
