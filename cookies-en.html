1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website saves on your device. They are used to make the site work, remember preferences, maintain sessions, measure performance, and improve the overall user experience.

  • Preference Cookies: These remember settings such as language, display preferences, or site configuration.
  • Analytics / Performance Cookies: These help us understand how the site is used (pages visited, errors, load times) to improve stability and experience.
